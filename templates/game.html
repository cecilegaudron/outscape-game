The game

OUTscape game, what is it ? How to play ?

Whether you are with family, friends, partners or colleagues, our OUTscape game is for Berliners and visitors alike. Take advantage of this investigation to glean clues in Berlin-Mitte.

Practical Info

Start the game at Alexanderplatz where one of our investigators will give you the necessary equipment to solve the investigation.
